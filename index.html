Linkal

2018C

Linkal is a game developed during a special week at IUT Annecy, in group we had to develop a game in C in one week.

In Linkal, you have 7 levels, to finish each level, you have to go on the flag. To do this, you can use portals thats you can only place on light grey blocs.
You spawn in the light-blue base, and you die if you go in the lava (the red blocs). You can put a blue portal and an orange portal and cross them. You can also take a blue cube and put him on buttons to activate some mechanisms.
